**Okinawa Homeless help service**

CTD Japan is a youth-led, community-driven initiative aimed at navigating homelessness and housing insecurity through accessible technology.
We believe in empowering individuals by connecting them directly to nearby resources (shower facilities, food pantries, shelters, and counseling services) in Japan.

Our app is designed for ease of use, with no registration, no payments, and no information collected that can be tied back to you. We strictly use your location to pinpoint the closest help.
